The devices on our wrists and in our pockets must work for us, not for software and media companies. They must be designed, therefore, to help us live according to our own values, not to lead us towards things that they want to maximize, like time spent scrolling, app downloads, or ad revenue. Read our manifesto.

Will & Intent is a small organization that faciliates discussion and discernment around the values behind the design of our notification centers, our app stores, etc. We'll fund projects that:

  • Collect and publish open data about software use and alignment with end-user values. (see, the Collective Experience Project and a chrome extension called Hindsight)
  • Increase articulacy about value-alignment amongst designers. (see Human Values, Choicemaking, and Interface Design, a design patterns handbook)
  • Increase articulacy about value-alignment amongst the internet leadership. (see Doubt Club SF — a monthly event for leaders in SF tech to think philosophically)
  • Create demos and communities that advance the cause of a nonmanipulative internet.
