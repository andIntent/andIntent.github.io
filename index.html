We are a loose affiliation of designers, philosophers, economists, and operating systems researchers with an ambitious vision: Operating systems that understand and prioritize the individual values of each user, rather than the values of software companies, media companies, or app entrepreneurs.

We create demos and small projects to light the way for industry and for policymakers, and to excite users. We're starting with redesigns for top level interfaces—home screens, notification centers, app stores, and browsers—and with data models to enable these redesigns.

Projects

  • A Values Taxonomy
  • The Collective Experience Project — the world's only database of the values behind and outcomes of user engagements with various URLs
  • Hindsight — a chrome extension for matching web use to your underlying values and goals
  • Letting Users Choose According to their Own Values — a new Human Interface Guidelines for Human Choicemaking
  • Doubt Club SF — a monthly event for tech CEOs interested in reexamining their impact
